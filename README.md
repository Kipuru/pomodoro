# Kipuru's Pomodoro Timer
This is a small app I decided to make to act as my personal ideal Pomodoro timer. A secondary motivation was to practice programming and creation of coding projects.
## What is the "Pomodoro Technique"?
The Pomodoro Technique is a time management method commonly used by students like me to study or do work. Its basic principle is to divide work into sections of 25 minutes of work time, followed by 5 minutes of break, looped over and over again.
